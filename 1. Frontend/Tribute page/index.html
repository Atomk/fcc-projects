Leonardo da Vinci

The most notable polymath in history

A small list of facts about Leonardo:

  • He was born on 15 April 1452
  • During his life, he was among the best in almost every field of study.
  • His areas of interest included invention, painting, sculpting, architecture, science, music, mathematics, engineering, literature, anatomy, geology, astronomy, botany, writing, history, and cartography.
  • He was considered "a man who can do all things if he will" by people of his time.
  • He is considered one of the best painters of all time.
  • He was a vegetarian.
  • It took da Vinci about ten years to paint Mona Lisa's lips.
